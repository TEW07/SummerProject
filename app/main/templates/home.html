Welcome to the Flashcard Creation Application!

This app is designed to help you create, manage, and review flashcards effectively. By utilising the biweekly Leitner Review Schedule, you'll be able to maximise your learning retention through spaced repetition and retrieval practice.

The Leitner Review System

The Leitner System is a well-established method for organising your study sessions in a way that prioritises both efficiency and effective memory retention. The system divides your flashcards into different "decks" or "boxes" based on how well you know the material. Here's how it works:

Each deck represents a stage in the review process. When you correctly answer a card during a review session, it moves to the next deck, which means it will be reviewed less frequently. Conversely, if you answer a card incorrectly, it moves back to the Deck 1.

Therefore, the cards you struggle with are reviewed more often, while those you know well are reviewed less frequently. This approach allows you to allocate your time where itâ€™s needed most.
